Meet Our Team

The passionate artisans, roasters, and baristas behind every perfect cup.

Lucas Miller

Founder & Sourcing Lead

Lucas travels to high-altitude farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Sumatra to build direct-trade relationships with coffee farmers. He ensures every harvest is ethically sourced and of outstanding quality.

🔥

Maya Chen

Head Roaster

Maya is a master of heat, airflow, and time. She monitors bean behavior in the roaster, engineering precise temperature curves to unlock complex origins and preserve natural sweetness.

🎨

Marcus Vance

Lead Barista & Educator

Marcus manages our bar flow and trains our staff in extraction physics and milk texturing. An award-winning latte artist, he is dedicated to serving visual and sensory perfection.
